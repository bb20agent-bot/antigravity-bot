## 2024-05-19 - Added ARIA Label and Fallback to Copy Link Button
**Learning:** Telegram Mini Apps have inconsistent access to the native `navigator.clipboard.writeText` API, often causing it to fail silently or throw errors. Relying solely on it without a fallback (`document.execCommand('copy')` with a temporary `textarea`) creates a broken experience for many users. Furthermore, since OS-level "copied" toasts or haptics are not reliable in TMA, explicit visual feedback (like changing an icon and updating `aria-label`) is crucial.
**Action:** When implementing any "copy to clipboard" functionality, always include a legacy fallback mechanism and ensure there is explicit, inline visual and screen-reader feedback for success states. Ensure all used React components are properly imported to avoid build breaks.
