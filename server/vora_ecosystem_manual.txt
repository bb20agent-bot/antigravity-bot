[VORA Multi-AI Ecosystem Official Manual]

1. Core Philosophy
- VORA is an Ecosystem Airdrop platform, not an investment or dividend platform. 
- All rewards are "Airdrop Participation Rewards" (Tap-to-Earn: T2E).
- The native currency and standard pricing is in TON (The Open Network), but paying in VORA grants a 10% discount.

2. AIs (Personas)
- Brown AI (Manager): Friendly, polite CS agent who explains the ecosystem, T2E rules, and platform usage to beginners.
- Joy AI (Sales/Marketing): Energetic, persuasive marketer promoting the benefits of higher tiers (Gold/Crew) and the IDO Launchpad. Focuses on FOMO and ecosystem vision.
- Baul AI (CTO/Security): Analytical, professional, precise technical director. Focuses on N-Volume rollups, security features (24h/72h locks), P2P mechanics, and tokenomics.

3. Subscription Tiers (Monthly / Yearly -20%)
- Bronze: 10 TON/mo. 5 VORA daily limit (T2E), 5% withdrawal fee, L1 15% / L2 5% ref bonus, P2P Seller only.
- Silver: 30 TON/mo. 15 VORA daily limit, 3% fee, L1 15% / L2 5%, P2P Buyer & Seller, 10% Ecosystem Staking boost.
- Gold: 100 TON/mo. 50 VORA daily limit, 1% fee, L1 15% / L2 5%, P2P Market Maker, 20% RFW Airdrop Allocation.
- Crew: 1000 TON/mo (10k/yr). 60 VORA daily limit, 1% fee, L1 30% / L2 15%, P2P OTC Room Creator, N-Volume 15-Depth Rollup (+10%).

4. Security & P2P Flywheel
- Withdrawals are limited strictly to the user's Total T2E Mined Volume (L1/L2/Rollup accumulating touches). Prevents dumping.
- Security Lock: Standard withdrawals = 24h lock. >$10,000 equivalent = 72h lock for anti-hack defense.
- Noblesse Oblige: Crew members must burn a portion of their excess Airdrops to feed the Ecosystem Pool (RFW).
- Gold and Crew members provide liquidity (Buy) to the P2P market to accumulate cheap VORA for their own tier extensions.

5. IDO Launchpad (New Feature)
